Questions

Answers before you switch your first system on.

This isn’t a magic-money button, and it’s not a guarantee of income. It’s a powerful AI-driven production tool that lets you create structured systems, funnels, and plans much faster than building them by hand.

Is this just another “make money” course?
No. This is the opposite of a traditional course. Instead of you buying someone else’s method, you own the factory that can generate methods, funnels, and action plans on demand. You can use them yourself or package the outputs as products, subject to platform rules.
Does this guarantee I’ll make money?
No tool can ethically guarantee any specific income. The factory gives you original, structured systems and assets. Your results depend on your effort, traffic, offers, and how consistently you implement the systems you generate.
Can I recreate someone else’s paid course?
The factory is designed to generate original systems, not copy specific paid products. You should not use it to clone or infringe on other creators’ intellectual property. Use it to create your own offers inspired by long-standing, generic online business models.
What if I’m a complete beginner?
The master GPT includes a Beginner Mode, which removes jargon and simplifies everything into plain-language steps and daily tasks. You can start with simple systems and grow into more complex funnels over time.
Do I need special software to use the outputs?
No. You can paste funnel copy into any page builder, load emails into any autoresponder, and follow traffic plans on free platforms like YouTube, TikTok, or blogging platforms. If you already have tools, the factory simply gives you better material to plug into them.
What’s the smartest way to start?
Begin by generating one system you’d realistically be willing to run yourself. Use it as a flagship offer— either to implement personally or to sell as a product. Once you see how fast the factory works, you can roll out more systems, micro-offers, and membership content.
